5. What are the charges for shipping?

Shipping cost refers to the cost of shipping an item from the origin to the destination country and the same needs to be paid to the courier partners.

We offer Free Shipping for all orders above 150 USD. All shipments below 150 USD will be charged 25 USD.

6. How do International Duties and Taxes work?

Every time an item crosses an international border from one country to another, it is subject to these charges by the importing country's rules and regulations.

Charges such as Custom or Import Duties, VAT, Local Sales Tax, and Clearance charges are not in our or any seller's control and are determined by the respective local government bodies.

Hence, for all international orders, customers will be solely responsible for bearing the Customs duty and other clearance formalities.
